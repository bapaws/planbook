First complete, then perfect.
Planbook is a productivity app that seamlessly integrates task management, note-taking, and journaling. Manage your time scientifically and document your life elegantly.

【Smart & Simple | Intelligent Classification】
● Eisenhower Matrix (4-quadrant) for prioritizing tasks
● Multi-level tag system for flexible organization
● Inbox captures ideas instantly—never miss a to-do
● Recurring tasks help build lasting habits

【Complete & Comprehensive | Full Task Details】
● View all task attributes at a glance: time, priority, tags
● Break down big goals with subtasks
● Auto-link notes when completing tasks for easy review
● Image attachments for richer information

【Clear & Organized | Timeline View】
● Day/Week/Month calendar perspectives
● Timeline displays your entire day's tasks and notes
● Morning, afternoon, and evening segments

【Quick Search | Hierarchical Tags】
● Multi-level tag hierarchy for notes and tasks
● Filter by Work, Study, Life with one tap
● Show or hide completed tasks

【Journal | Flip Through Your Fulfilling Life】
● Daily reviews to track your growth
● Task completion statistics to see your progress
● Note timeline—pen the chapters of your life
